Hold on your hats it's about to get real picklish, stick with is as we explore the great world of pickles!

Pickles are the creating on Samule B Pickling, this noted schoolar was at home one rainy afternoon and decided " I'm gonna pickle some pickles ", and boom pickles were birthed.

Now pickles have a simple but genius origin, but what most don't know is the sad story of the creators death. One hot morning Samule B. Pickling decided to try and pickle an onion, having had such great success with his pickled pickle invention. He started by prepping his work station, when suddenly he tripped falling mouth first into a carelessly left behind pickle. Poor Samuel B Pickling died choking on his pickle, found by his widow the Dutchess Pickling. In his honor his sons Rick and Morty carried his legacy on, adding new members to the Pickling family.
